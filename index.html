Cahoot

Cahoot makes deciding what to do with your friends simpler.

Search for an activity (“Drinks, Lower East Side”), invite your friends, and each invited friend is presented with options they can “Like” or “Dislike.” The most liked place is where your group goes.

  • Date: January 2015
  • Category: iOS Application

Ping - Your Group Finding Companion

Ping allows you to share your location with any group of friends you create by simply tapping a button.

Trying to find where your friends are at on a busy weekend night? Simply create a group with them and start sharing your location whenever you want.

  • Date: January 2015
  • Category: iOS Application
